Head Socket — Round 2 (seating / profile study)

📖 Round runbook →

The team-wide right-temple socket, re-rendered with the new set-in construction — a raised rim that is always the highest edge, with the field the sockets sit in at one of three levels. Answers Dimitri's r1 'relief too proud' note. Two shapes (P1 incumbent triangle, P6 mecha-wolf); P1 also tried with a centre 'U-I' gang emblem. Engines: flash31 + gptimage2med. Verdicts are fresh-context crop audits; the human eye-call is open.

Pre-read. The headline decision is the field level: flush (face at skin level), recessed (~0.3–0.5 mm dug in), or raised (~0.3–0.5 mm proud plateau) — the rim stands proud above the field in all three. Judge which seating reads best against the skin. Secondary: P1 vs P6 for the mark, and whether the incumbent triangle carries the U-I centre emblem (IP-checked, no clash found). Size stays tunable at authoring (the other half of the r1 note). Delivery is an authored STL fused per character, so field level and size are free parameters — judge the read, not the exact millimetres.
