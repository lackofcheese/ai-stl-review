Goal

Take Mace's best mesh (m2) and clean up the botched temple socket so the head is ready to receive the real gang mark. The reconstruction turned her triangular three-pin socket into a soft blob; this round removes that defective relief and rebuilds a smooth, watertight temple patch in its place — a clean surface the new head-socket asset can be grafted onto without gaps or holes. Nothing else about the model changes. This is the prep step that unblocks putting the *real* socket on Mace.

This round is the producer side of head-socket r3's fuse step (which is the consumer): r3 authors the wolf master and boolean-unions it onto a *manifold* temple — r9 is what makes Mace's temple manifold and clean.
