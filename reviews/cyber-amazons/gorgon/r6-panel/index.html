Round-6 systematics (what this round taught)

  • Self-edit img2img is the base-fix mechanism: feed the base model its own output + "exactly N changes" — moved a reference-level property (hair side) that per-panel text lost 12/12 at t1/t2, twice, for $0.36/call.
  • The hair chronic is solved by a dedicated negative: r5's one-line ask went 0/9; this round's physical-assembly block + "NOT down her back" went 4/4 on riverflow (both arms) and 5/9 overall.
  • Camera-via-img2img softens (r2 said impossible): an explicit numbered ~20° orbit transferred on flash31 (~25–30°) and riverflow (~20–30°), froze on gptimage2 (0°), inverted on seedream45 (rotated to her back). Model-dependent, modest orbits only.
  • pro2k mirrored an img2img reference — first counterexample to "pixels beat the attractor" (and it flipped AWAY from its own measured rightward lock). Chirality-reliability strike; README dossier updated.
  • Cross-model editing of another model's output is a lottery (1/5): keep edits in-family — the base's own model first, Gemini second.
  • New text-regen systematic: with the camera more frontal, the armored knee pad + shin guard bound to the BARE leg 2/2 in arm A — future text regens need the pads tied to the LEGGING, not to position words.
