GORGON — 10-model concept panel

Jason picked Gorgon (Blocker #2) as the next model. Round 1 = photoreal-first front-view concept (the track that won on Mace r2/r3): one pinned prompt through the 10-model panel, then a fresh-context audit that cropped and 2×-zoomed head/torso/hands/feet on every image. Spec: tall V-tapered juicer, both arms sleek mil-spec chrome, cable-dreads swept back, three-pin socket at her right temple (viewer's left in front view), stiff-arm advance, both feet flat.

Verdict wanted — two equally valid answers: (1) Pick a DESIGN KEEPER (Claude pre-read: riverflow25, the only chirality-correct one) and optionally a POSE/ENERGY reference (pre-read: pro2k); or (2) reject the whole panel and just give feedback — 'none of these; open the stance up, more mass in the shoulders, different dread length' etc. is every bit as useful, and goes straight into the r1.5 prompt for a fresh sweep. With a keeper, r2 converts it to monochrome clay via flash31. Either way the next round also fixes the three systematic misses at prompt level: the temple socket (9/10 wrong side/shape/place), the stiff-arm collapsing to a bent 'stop' gesture (7/10), and raised rear heels vs the flat-plant basing rule (6/10). Jersey #4 is provisional — veto welcome.
